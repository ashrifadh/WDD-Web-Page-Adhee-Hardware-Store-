HOW TO USE:
-----------

SEARCH PRODUCTS:
- Type in the search bar to find products by name
- Search also works for categories

FILTER BY CATEGORY:
- Use the dropdown to show only specific categories
- Select "All Categories" to show everything

MANAGE PRODUCTS:
- Edit: Update product details (name, price, stock, image)
- Stock: Quickly update stock quantity
- Supplier: Assign or change supplier
- Delete: Remove product from inventory

VISUAL INDICATORS:
- Red badge = Low stock (below minimum level)
- Green badge = In stock (sufficient quantity)
- Yellow category badge = Product category
- Truck icon = Supplier information

RESPONSIVE DESIGN:
- Desktop: 3-4 products per row
- Tablet: 2-3 products per row
- Mobile: 1 product per row
